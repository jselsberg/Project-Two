Why Join Koaches 4 Kids?

"I volunteered as a basketball coach four semesters during my time at Wash U. I can honestly say that it has consistently been the highlight of every week I have participated.

It is just as rewarding to see the younger kids fall in love with the game as it is to teach older kids some of the nuances of basketball, such as pick-and-rolls, the zone defense, and boxing out. On top of it all, game days are a blast! I get very into every game."

"I've been involved in youth sports with the St. Louis YMCA every semester so far at Wash U and am completely in love with it. I've coached soccer, volleyball, and flag football and each one has been a ton of fun.

I've enjoyed co-coaching with girls in my sorority as a way to bond. I have a busy schedule, so its great that coaching only involves an hour of practice and one game per week and isn't a huge commitment. Koaches 4 Kids has truly made me feel like a part of the St. Louis community. Plus, the kids are so cute and so fun to be around!"
