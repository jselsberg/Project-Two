What is Koaches 4 Kids?

Koaches 4 Kids, lead by the Wash U Campus Y, is an opportunity to coach a little league sports team in conjunction with a local YMCA. Plan and leading practices once-a-week and coach weekly games. There are opportunities to coach children as young as 4-5 years old to as old as fourth and fifth graders. Coach a team with a group of friends, lead a team by yourself, or meet other up Wash U students by coaching a team together. There are seasons during the fall and spring semesters. If you happen to be around St. Louis, you can coach during the winter and summer as well.

Sports available to coach include:

  • Basketball
  • Soccer
  • Baseball, Softball, and T-Ball
  • Flag Football
  • Volleyball
  • Gymnastics and Dance
